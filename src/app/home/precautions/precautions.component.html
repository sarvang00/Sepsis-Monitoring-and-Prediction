PRECAUTIONS

i) Sepsis is a serious condition.
ii) Make sure to keep the following things in mind while acting with the patient.
iii) Report the supervising attending and resident as soon as possible.
iv) Approach the patient with face mask always on.
v) Maintain strict hygiene. Regularly clense your hands with sanitizer.
vi) All the sterilizable metal devices operated upon patient should be sterilized twice.
vii) Dispozable parts must be disposed off in ' Biohazard' bin.
viii) Strict isolation of patient must be implemented to prevent infection spread and more bacterial attack on patient itself.
